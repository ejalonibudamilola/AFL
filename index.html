About Me

Apostle Femi Lazarus is the lead pastor of Sphere of Light Church. He is an Apostle with a prophetic cutting edge. He has the mandate to raise God's end time armies. Apostle Femi Lazarus is a public health professional by training. He is the principal of the international School of Ministry, a platform through which he has trained ministers from more than thirty nations. He is a lover of God. He is married to Pastor Miracle Femi-Lazarus and they are blessed with a daughter Abigail Lazarus.
